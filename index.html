Users already swap data informally on WhatsApp and Telegram, but these swaps are unstructured, unsafe, and unreliable. The demand exists. The infrastructure doesn't.

Our Solution
Intra-network swaps
Users swap data directly via existing telecom USSD (MTN↔MTN, Airtel↔Airtel, etc.). We don't touch the data.
Simple pricing
Flat platform fee of ₦20-50 per successful swap based on data size. Charged only after both users confirm completion.
No wallet, no escrow
We provide the discovery, matching, and confirmation layer only. This structure avoids fintech and telecom licensing requirements.
Campus-first distribution
Launching at 5 pilot universities with student ambassadors who get free swaps + ₦50 per referral. CAC: ₦200-500 vs ₦2,000+ for paid ads.
What's Next

Now — Pre-MVP

Technical specifications complete (FlutterFlow PWA + Supabase backend). Payment integration tested. 15 beta testers recruited. First 3 proof swaps completed with 100% success rate.

Q1 2025 — 3-Month Pilot

Launch at 5 universities (UI, UNN, OAU, UNILAG, UNIBEN). Target: 500 users, 1,000+ swaps, >80% completion rate. Goal: Prove unit economics.

Mid-2025 — National Expansion

Replicate to 20 campuses, then 50. Path to $250K ARR in Year 1 with 30,000 swaps/day (less than 0.03% of Nigeria's internet subscribers).

2026+ — Pan-African

Same model works across Africa (Ghana, Kenya, South Africa). Platform enables adjacent verticals: bill-splitting, group buying, micro-financial services for students.
